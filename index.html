Shared Prosperity

At VReality, we believe that prosperity should be shared. That's why we've designed our profit-sharing mechanism to benefit not only our company but also our loyal community of VRLT token holders.

Reinvesting Profits for Expansion

50% of the profits generated from our technology projects are reinvested back into the company. This allocation allows us to scale our operations, expand our team, and bring our ambitious projects to fruition on a larger scale.


Fueling Tomorrow's Technology

30% of profits are earmarked for innovation and marketing initiatives. This allocation ensures that we continue to push the boundaries of technological innovation and reach new audiences who can benefit from our products and services.


Token Burn Strategy

The remaining 20% of profits are used to buy back VRLT tokens from the market, which are subsequently burned. This process not only reduces the circulating supply of VRLT tokens but also increases their scarcity, thereby driving up their value. As a result, our VReality community stands to benefit from the appreciation of their holdings.

VReality(VRLT) Coin

Far beyond a mere cryptocurrency, the VReality (VRLT) token embodies our dedication to pioneering innovation and driving progress. Supported by our cutting-edge technology initiatives, the VRLT token represents an investment in tomorrow's advancements. By choosing to invest in VRLT, you're not simply acquiring a token; you're embracing a vibrant community and actively fueling the expansion of our transformative projects.
